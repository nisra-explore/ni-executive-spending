View a Quick read

Quick read gives you a fast and easy summary of Public Expenditure. If you dont have time (or just dont feel like digging into the details), this is where youll find the key points at a glance. No fuss, just the essentials!

For those interested in exploring the data in greater detail, you can download it using the button above. The download includes all the data from the web pages, along with additional breakdowns of DEL and AME, as well as resource and capital information. Alternatively, you can view a specific page related to capital spend or resource spend.
